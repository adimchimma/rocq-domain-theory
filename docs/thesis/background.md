I first became immersed in the world of **Proof Assistants** (computer programs that help verify mathematical proofs are correct), specifically **Rocq**, through an **REU** (Research Experience for Undergraduates) at the University of Pennsylvania. It was also there that I learned about the field of **Domain Theory** (a mathematical framework for understanding computation), which aligned perfectly with my interests at the intersection of Mathematics and Computer Science. This thesis serves as an opportunity to expand my interests as well as develop my knowledge base.
